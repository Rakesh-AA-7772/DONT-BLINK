Rules & Instructions

  1. Match your real eyes with the on-screen avatar — keep them aligned during the run.
  2. Do not leave the camera zone; stepping away immediately stops the timer and will not submit a score.
  3. Remove spectacles or sunglasses for accurate detection.
  4. Ensure adequate lighting (avoid backlight). Recommended: well-lit face with average brightness.
  5. Keep your face centered and roughly at the same distance from the camera for the duration.
  6. A blink (detected by MediaPipe EAR) ends the run — move minimally and avoid rapid head turns.
  7. No external occlusions (hands, objects) between your face and the camera.
  8. Do not cover or obstruct the camera lens during a run.
  9. Use the enabled “Enable Camera” control if browser blocks autoplay; allow camera permissions.
  10. Scores captured while leaving the zone are considered invalid (marked as cheating) and will not be auto-submitted.
  11. Play fairly — leaderboard entries should reflect genuine runs only.
